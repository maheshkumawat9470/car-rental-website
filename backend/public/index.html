Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers to help you get on the road.

What documents do I need to rent a vehicle?

You need a valid driving license, a government-issued ID (like Aadhar or Passport), and a credit/debit card for the security deposit.

Is fuel included in the rental price?

No, fuel is not included. You will receive the vehicle with a certain fuel level, and you are expected to return it with the same level.

How does the security deposit work?

A flat refundable security deposit of ₹5000 is required at the time of booking. It is refunded within 3-5 business days after you return the vehicle in good condition.

Can I cancel or modify my booking?

Yes, free cancellation is available up to 24 hours before your pickup time. Modifications depend on vehicle availability.
